Chuyển lời nói trực tiếp sang câu tường thuật

Reported speech xuất hiện rất nhiều trong writing, reading và speaking. Module này giúp bạn nhớ cách đổi thì, đổi đại từ và đổi trạng từ thời gian khi tường thuật lời nói của người khác.
Direct speechReported speechTense shiftTime words
Cách học: Xác định chủ ngữ, động từ tường thuật, rồi lùi thì và đổi đại từ nếu cần.

Quy tắc nhanh

  • “I am tired,” she said → She said that she was tired.
  • “We will go,” they said → They said that they would go.
  • “I met him yesterday,” she said → She said that she had met him the day before.
  • Đổi today → that day, tomorrow → the next day, now → then.
1
Direct: “I can help you,” he said.
2
Reported: He said that he could help me.

Mini practice

Chuyển câu sau sang reported speech:

She said, “I am learning English.”

Đáp án mẫu: She said that she was learning English.

Doi tu chi thoi gian/noi chon

today→ that day
tomorrow→ the next day / the following day
yesterday→ the day before / the previous day
now→ then / at that time
here→ there
this→ that
these→ those
ago→ before / earlier

Reported Questions/Commands

  • Yes/No question: asked if/whether
    "Do you like it?" → He asked if I liked it.
  • Wh-question: asked + wh + S + V (khong dao)
    "Where do you live?" → She asked where I lived.
  • Command: told + O + to V
    "Sit down!" → She told me to sit down.
  • Request: asked + O + to V
    "Please help me." → He asked me to help him.
  • Negative: told + O + not to V
    "Don't run!" → She told them not to run.

Loi hay gap

  • Quen lui thi.
  • Khong doi dai tu (I → he/she).
  • Quen doi trang tu thoi gian (today → that day).
  • Giu nguyen dau ngoac kep nhu cau truc tiep.
  • Dao ngu trong reported questions: Where did I live (SAI) → where I lived.

Vi sao quan trong

  • Rat quan trong cho reading comprehension va writing.
  • Giup ban ke lai thong tin mot cach tu nhien.
  • La nen cho cau gian tiep nang cao.
  • Hay gap trong IELTS Listening khi ke lai noi dung.
