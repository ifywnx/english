5 KỸ THUẬT PARAPHRASE

Tổng quan kỹ thuật

Paraphrase = viết lại ý tưởng bằng từ ngữ khác, KHÔNG thay đổi nghĩa gốc. Đây là cách tránh lặp từ và chứng minh vốn từ vựng phong phú.

1. Synonyms

Thay từ bằng từ đồng nghĩa: important → crucial, vital, essential

2. Word Form

Đổi loại từ: pollution (n) → pollute (v) → polluted (adj)

3. Đổi cấu trúc câu

Thay đổi vị trí chủ-vị: "X causes Y" → "Y is caused by X"

4. Active ↔ Passive

"People consume..." → "...is consumed by people"

2. Đổi Word Form

Gốc: The pollution of rivers is a serious problem.
Paraphrase: Rivers that are polluted pose a significant threat. (noun → adjective)
Gốc: The development of technology has changed our lives.
Paraphrase: Technology has developed rapidly, transforming our daily lives. (noun → verb)

3. Đổi cấu trúc câu

Gốc: Many people believe that education is the key to success.
Paraphrase: Education is widely regarded as the foundation of achievement.
Gốc: Social media has a negative impact on young people.
Paraphrase: Young people are adversely affected by social media platforms.

4. Active ↔ Passive

Active: The government should ban plastic bags.
Passive: Plastic bags should be banned by the authorities.

VÍ DỤ PARAPHRASE TRONG IELTS

Task 1 — Paraphrase đề bài

Đề: The graph shows the number of visitors to three London museums between 2007 and 2012.
Paraphrase: The line graph illustrates visitor figures for three museums in London over a five-year period from 2007 to 2012.
Đề: The chart below shows the percentage of households owning a car in a European country.
Paraphrase: The bar chart compares the proportion of families with car ownership in a European nation.

Task 2 — Paraphrase thesis

Đề: Some people think that the government should spend more money on public services rather than on the arts.
Paraphrase: It is argued that state funding should be directed towards essential services instead of cultural and artistic endeavours.

Reading — Nhận biết paraphrase

Trong IELTS Reading, đáp án thường là paraphrase của passage. Nhận biết paraphrase = tìm đáp án nhanh hơn.

Passage nóiCâu hỏi/đáp án paraphrase
"a significant number of people""many individuals"
"the research was conducted""the study was carried out"
"prior to the industrial revolution""before industrialization began"
"had a detrimental effect on""negatively impacted"

BÀI TẬP PARAPHRASE

Luyện tập — Viết paraphrase

Đọc câu gốc → viết lại bằng từ khác → bấm "Xem gợi ý" để so sánh.

Câu 1: Technology has changed the way people communicate with each other.
The manner in which individuals interact has been fundamentally transformed by technological advancements.
Câu 2: Many students prefer to study abroad rather than in their own country.
A considerable number of learners opt for overseas education over domestic institutions.
Câu 3: The government should invest more money in public transportation.
Greater financial resources should be allocated by the authorities towards enhancing public transit systems.
Câu 4: Air pollution is a major problem in many large cities around the world.
Atmospheric contamination poses a significant challenge in numerous metropolitan areas globally.
Câu 5: Children who spend too much time playing computer games may have problems in school.
Excessive engagement with video games among young learners can adversely affect their academic performance.
