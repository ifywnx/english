Writing Task 1

Mô tả bản đồ — Map Description

Dạng bài Map yêu cầu bạn so sánh sự thay đổi của một khu vực qua 2 thời điểm (trước/sau) hoặc mô tả bố trí không gian. Đây là dạng ít gặp nhưng dễ ghi điểm nếu nắm vững cấu trúc.
MapBefore/AfterLocationTask 1
Mẹo: Luôn bắt đầu bằng overview — nêu thay đổi lớn nhất trước, chi tiết sau.

Cấu trúc bài viết

  • Introduction: Paraphrase đề bài (1-2 câu).
  • Overview: Nêu 2-3 thay đổi chính — QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT.
  • Body 1: Mô tả khu vực ở thời điểm đầu.
  • Body 2: Mô tả thay đổi ở thời điểm sau.

Từ vựng quan trọng

  • Vị trí: in the north/south, adjacent to, opposite, next to
  • Thay đổi: was replaced by, was converted into, was demolished
  • Xây dựng: was constructed, was built, was developed
  • Mở rộng: was extended, was expanded, doubled in size

Lỗi thường gặp

  • Không có overview — mất điểm nặng.
  • Liệt kê quá chi tiết mà không nhóm ý.
  • Dùng thì sai (phải dùng past tense cho before/after).
  • Không dùng passive voice khi mô tả xây dựng.

Mẫu câu hay

  • "The most striking change is that..."
  • "The area to the east was transformed into..."
  • "A car park was constructed where the forest had been."
  • "Overall, the village became significantly more urbanised."

Bài mẫu band 7+ (Map)

De: The maps show a small town in 1990 and 2020.

Introduction: The two maps illustrate the changes that took place in a small town between 1990 and 2020.

Overview: Overall, the town underwent significant urbanisation over the 30-year period. The most notable changes were the replacement of farmland with residential areas and the construction of new infrastructure.

Body 1: In 1990, the town was predominantly rural, with farmland occupying the northern and eastern sections. A small cluster of houses was located in the centre, and a river ran through the southern part of the town.

Body 2: By 2020, the farmland in the north had been replaced by a large housing estate, and a shopping centre was constructed in the east. A new bridge was built across the river, connecting the town centre to a newly developed sports complex in the south.
