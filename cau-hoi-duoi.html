Học đuôi câu hỏi như isn't it, don't you, did they

Tag questions thường dùng trong giao tiếp tự nhiên để xác nhận thông tin hoặc kéo người nghe vào cuộc trò chuyện. Đây là phần nhỏ nhưng rất hay gặp trong speaking.
Positive tagNegative tagSpeakingConversation
Cách học: Câu khẳng định thường đi với tag phủ định, câu phủ định thường đi với tag khẳng định.

Quy tắc nhanh

  • It’s cold, isn’t it?
  • You like coffee, don’t you?
  • She went home, didn’t she?
  • They are coming, aren’t they?
1
Positive + negative tag — You are ready, aren’t you?
2
Negative + positive tag — He isn’t late, is he?

Mini practice

Viết tag question cho câu sau: You can swim.

Đáp án mẫu: You can swim, can’t you?

Giải thích: Câu khẳng định đi với tag phủ định.

Bang tag questions theo thi

Cau chinhTagVi du
Present Simple (is/are)isn't/aren'tShe is happy, isn't she?

Past Simpledidn'tHe went home, didn't he?
Present Perfecthasn't/haven'tThey have left, haven't they?

Negative sentencepositive tagHe can't swim, can he?

Truong hop dac biet

  • I am → aren't I? (KHONG phai "am I not")
    I am right, aren't I?
  • Let's → shall we?
    Let's go, shall we?
  • Nobody/nothing/no one (phu dinh) → positive tag
    Nobody came, did they?
  • Imperative → will you? / won't you?
    Close the door, will you?
  • There is/are → isn't/aren't there?
    There is a book, isn't there?

Ngu dieu tag question

  • Rising intonation ↗: Ban that su KHONG BIET va can xac nhan.
    You're coming, aren't you? ↗ (hoi that)
  • Falling intonation ↘: Ban DA BIET va chi muon xac nhan lai.
    Nice day, isn't it? ↘ (small talk)
  • Trong IELTS Speaking, dung tag question de them tu nhien.
  • Tranh dung tag question qua nhieu trong van viet.

Lỗi hay gặp

  • Dung sai tro dong tu trong tag.
  • Khong khop voi thi cua cau chinh.
  • Quen dao phu dinh / khang dinh.
  • Dung tag question qua guong trong van viet.
  • Nham chu ngu: Everyone/Nobody dung they.

Vì sao module này quan trọng

  • Rat hay gap trong speaking tu nhien.
  • Giup cau noi mem hon va co tinh tuong tac.
  • La phan nho nhung de ghi diem vi dung dung ngu canh.
  • Thuong gap trong IELTS Listening.
