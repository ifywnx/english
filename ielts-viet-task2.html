IELTS Writing

Viết bài luận Task 2 theo từng dạng đề để không bị lạc ý

Task 2 không chỉ là viết đủ 250 từ. Quan trọng hơn là nhận diện dạng bài, chọn khung lập luận đúng, và phát triển từng body paragraph rõ ràng. Module này chia nhỏ theo format đề thật để bạn học dễ hơn.
Opinion Discussion Problem / Solution Advantages / Disadvantages
How to learn: Xác định dạng đề trước, viết thesis ngắn, rồi dựng 2 body paragraph có topic sentence, explanation và example.

4 dạng đề phải nắm

1
Opinion — nêu quan điểm rõ ràng, đồng ý hay không đồng ý.
2
Discussion — thảo luận hai phía rồi đưa quan điểm của bạn.
3
Problem / Solution — nêu vấn đề và cách giải quyết.
4
Advantages / Disadvantages — phân tích mặt lợi và mặt hại.

Khung viết nhanh

1
Introduction: paraphrase đề + thesis.
2
Body 1: topic sentence + explanation + example.
3
Body 2: ý thứ hai hoặc phản biện.
4
Conclusion: tóm lại quan điểm, không thêm ý mới.

Mini practice: Viết dàn ý 2 body cho đề về online learning vs classroom learning.

Ngôn ngữ ăn điểm

Introduce

This essay will discuss..., The issue of... has become increasingly important.

Explain

One major reason is..., This happens because..., As a result...

Example

For instance, A clear example is..., This can be seen in...

Conclude

In conclusion, Overall, it is evident that..., To sum up...

Lỗi hay gặp

  • Trả lời sai trọng tâm của đề.
  • Thesis mơ hồ hoặc đổi lập trường giữa bài.
  • Body paragraph không có topic sentence rõ.
  • Conclusion thêm ý mới.

Vì sao module này quan trọng

  • Task 2 chiếm trọng số lớn trong IELTS Writing.
  • Giúp người học tư duy lập luận có cấu trúc.
  • Là nền để nâng band từ 5.x lên 6.5+.
Xem bài mẫu Kiểm tra bài viết Làm quiz

Sample Answer — Band 7+ (Opinion)

Topic: Some people think that universities should provide graduates with the knowledge and skills needed in the workplace. Others think that the true purpose of a university education is to give access to knowledge for its own sake. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Introduction: It is often debated whether higher education should primarily serve vocational purposes or focus on academic enrichment. While both perspectives have merit, I believe that a balanced approach combining practical skills with intellectual development is the most beneficial.

Body 1: On the one hand, proponents of workplace-oriented education argue that graduates need practical competencies to secure employment. For instance, engineering or medical students require hands-on training that directly applies to their careers. Without such preparation, graduates may struggle to meet industry demands, leading to unemployment or underperformance.

Body 2: On the other hand, universities have traditionally been centres of intellectual inquiry. Subjects such as philosophy, literature, and pure mathematics may not have immediate vocational applications, yet they develop critical thinking, creativity, and analytical skills. These transferable abilities are increasingly valued in a rapidly changing job market where specific technical skills can quickly become obsolete.

Conclusion: In conclusion, while vocational training is undeniably important, I believe universities should also preserve their role as institutions of broader learning. A curriculum that integrates practical skills with academic exploration produces well-rounded graduates who can adapt to future challenges.

Word count: ~210 | Band: 7+ | Type: Discuss + Opinion

Checklist truoc khi nop

  • Du 250 tu chua? (Tu 250-280 la ly tuong)
  • Introduction co paraphrase de + thesis ro rang?
  • Moi body paragraph co topic sentence?
  • Co explanation + example trong moi body?
  • Conclusion KHONG them y moi?
  • Su dung linking words tu nhien (However, Moreover, In addition)?
  • Kiểm tra loi chinh ta va ngu phap?

Linking words hay dung

  • Them y: Moreover, Furthermore, In addition, Besides
  • Tuong phan: However, Nevertheless, On the other hand, In contrast
  • Nguyen nhan: Because, Since, Due to, As a result
  • Vi du: For instance, For example, Such as, A clear example is
  • Ket luan: In conclusion, To sum up, Overall, All things considered
