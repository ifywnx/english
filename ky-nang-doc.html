Kỹ năng đọc hiểu

4 chiến lược đọc giúp bạn hiểu nhanh và chính xác hơn — áp dụng cho IELTS, TOEIC và đời thường.

Skimming
Doc luot de nam y chinh. Chi doc cau dau moi doan, tieu de va tu in dam.
Dung khi: Can biet bai viet noi ve gi trong 30 giay.
Scanning
Tim thong tin cu the: ten, so, ngay thang. Khong can doc ca bai.
Dung khi: Cau hoi yeu cau tim chi tiet cu the.
Inference
Suy luan y nghia tu ngu canh. Tac gia khong noi truc tiep nhung ban co the doan tu manh moi.
Dung khi: Cau hoi "What does the author imply?"
Identifying Main Idea
Xac dinh y chinh cua doan/bai. Thuong nam o cau dau hoac cau cuoi doan (topic sentence).
Dung khi: Cau hoi matching headings.
Tone & Attitude
Nhan biet thai do cua tac gia: ung ho, phan doi, trung lap, mai mia. Chu y tinh tu va trang tu.
Dung khi: "What is the author's attitude towards...?"

Luyen tap: Doc doan van va tra loi

The city of Melbourne, Australia, has been consistently ranked as one of the world's most liveable cities. Its diverse population of over five million residents enjoys excellent public transport, world-class healthcare, and a thriving cultural scene. The city is home to numerous parks and gardens, and its coffee culture is considered among the best globally. However, housing affordability remains a significant challenge, with property prices rising steadily over the past decade.

1. What is the main idea of the passage?
Melbourne is a highly liveable but expensive city
Melbourne has the best coffee in the world
Melbourne has over five million residents
Dung! Bai noi Melbourne rat liveable nhung housing affordability la challenge.
2. (Scanning) What is Melbourne's population?
Over 3 million
Over 5 million
Over 10 million
"Its diverse population of over five million residents"
3. (Inference) The author implies that Melbourne:
Is perfect with no problems
Has issues despite being generally great
Is too expensive to live in
"However" signals contrast — Melbourne rat tot nhung van co van de (housing).
