Câu chủ động và câu bị động

Module này giúp bạn hiểu khi nào dùng active voice, khi nào dùng passive voice, và cách chuyển đổi giữa hai dạng trong các thì cơ bản.
ActivePassiveGrammarWriting
Cách học: Tìm object của câu chủ động rồi đưa nó lên làm subject của câu bị động.

Quy tac nhanh

  • Active: The teacher explains the lesson.
  • Passive: The lesson is explained by the teacher.
  • Cong thuc: S + be + V3 (+ by agent)
  • Doi object cua active thanh subject cua passive

Active: People build houses here.

Passive: Houses are built here.

Mini practice

Chuyen cau sau sang bi dong:

They clean the room every day.

Dap an mau: The room is cleaned every day.

Bang chuyen doi 8 thi

ThiActivePassive
Present SimpleShe writes a letter.A letter is written.
Present ContinuousShe is writing a letter.A letter is being written.
Present PerfectShe has written a letter.A letter has been written.
Past SimpleShe wrote a letter.A letter was written.
Past ContinuousShe was writing a letter.A letter was being written.
Past PerfectShe had written a letter.A letter had been written.
Future SimpleShe will write a letter.A letter will be written.
Modal VerbsShe can write a letter.A letter can be written.

Khi nao dung Passive?

  • Khong biet ai lam: My bike was stolen. (ai an cap? khong biet)
  • Khong quan trong ai lam: English is spoken worldwide.
  • Van hoc thuat/khoa hoc: The experiment was conducted in 2020.
  • Tin tuc/bao chi: A new hospital has been built.
  • Lich su/formal: The law was passed in 1990.

Them vi du chuyen doi

  • Active: The dog bit the boy.
    Passive: The boy was bitten by the dog.
  • Active: They are building a bridge.
    Passive: A bridge is being built.
  • Active: Someone must fix this.
    Passive: This must be fixed.
  • Active: She had finished the report.
    Passive: The report had been finished.

Loi hay gap

  • Quen doi dong tu sang V3.
  • Khong khop thi cua to be.
  • Giu nguyen vi tri object ma khong chuyen len subject.
  • Them by... khong can thiet khi chu the khong quan trong.
  • Nham present perfect va past simple passive.

Vi sao quan trong

  • Rat hay gap trong writing hoc thuat.
  • Giup cau van khach quan hon.
  • La nen cho nhieu cau truc nang cao khac.
  • Hay xuat hien trong IELTS Writing Task 1.
