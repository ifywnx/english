Nghe chep chinh ta de bat tung am va tung tu

Dictation buoc ban nghe ky tung chi tiet cua cau: am cuoi, noi am, khoang dung. Day la cach hieu qua nhat de cai thien listening chinh xac va spelling trong thoi gian ngan.

Loi hay gap khi dictation

1. Nghe nhanh nhung khong chia cum.
2. Bo am cuoi (walked nghe thanh walk).
3. Nham noi am (an apple nghe thanh a napple).
4. Viet theo suy doan, khong bam am thanh.
5. Khong so transcript sau khi lam.

Vi sao dictation hieu qua

1. Giup tai nghe "sac" hon rat nhanh.
2. Tang kha nang nho cau truc tu nhien.
3. Ket hop tot voi shadowing.
4. Cai thien spelling va grammar dong thoi.
5. Chi can 15 phut/ngay la thay ket qua.
