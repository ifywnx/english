Phan biet True, False va Not Given that nhanh

Day la dang bai kho nhat IELTS Reading. Khong chi can hieu nghia, ma con phai biet khi nao thong tin co trong bai, khi nao bi phu dinh, va khi nao hoan toan khong duoc de cap.
IELTS Reading3 bai mauCham diem tu dong
Nguyen tac vang: Chi dua vao thong tin trong passage. Khong dung kien thuc ben ngoai de phan doan.
TRUE
Passage co thong tin KHOP NGHIA voi cau hoi (co the khac tu nhung cung y)
FALSE
Passage co thong tin TRAI NGUOC truc tiep voi cau hoi
NOT GIVEN
Khong co du thong tin de ket luan dung hay sai

Buoc 1: Doc cau hoi truoc

Gach chan tu khoa trong cau hoi. Tim vung passage chua tu khoa do.

Buoc 2: Locate trong passage

Tim doan van chua thong tin lien quan. Doc ky 2-3 cau xung quanh.

Buoc 3: So sanh tung cum tu

Doi chieu tung menh de cua cau hoi voi passage. Chu y synonyms va paraphrasing.

Buoc 4: Phan biet False vs NG

False = co bang chung trai nguoc. NG = khong co bang chung nao ca (ke ca trai nguoc).

Bay thuong gap

1Tu tuyet doi: always, never, all, only, every — thuong lam cau hoi thanh FALSE
2Synonyms: passage dung "essential", cau hoi dung "necessary" — van la TRUE
3Part match: passage chi noi mot phan, cau hoi noi toan bo — thuong la NG
4Comparison: passage noi A > B, cau hoi noi B > A — la FALSE
5Opinion vs Fact: tac gia noi "may" nhung cau hoi noi "definitely" — la FALSE

Bai 1: The History of Coffee

Coffee was first discovered in Ethiopia in the 9th century. A goat herder named Kaldi noticed that his goats became more energetic after eating berries from a certain tree. He shared his discovery with monks at a nearby monastery, who made a drink from the berries and found that it helped them stay awake during long evening prayers. By the 15th century, coffee was being cultivated in Yemen, and by the 16th century, it had spread to Egypt, Syria, and Turkey. The first European coffee house opened in Venice in 1645.

Bai 2: Sleep and Memory

Research conducted at Harvard University has shown that sleep plays a crucial role in memory consolidation. During deep sleep, the brain replays experiences from the day, strengthening neural connections that form memories. Studies have found that people who sleep for at least seven hours after learning new information retain significantly more than those who stay awake. However, the quality of sleep matters more than the quantity. Interrupted sleep can be as harmful as no sleep at all when it comes to memory formation.

Bai 3: Renewable Energy

Solar energy has become the fastest-growing source of renewable power globally. The cost of solar panels has dropped by approximately 90% since 2010, making it competitive with fossil fuels in many regions. China currently leads the world in solar panel manufacturing, producing over 70% of the global supply. While solar energy is clean and abundant, it faces challenges related to energy storage and intermittency. Wind energy, the second most popular renewable source, complements solar well because wind tends to blow more at night when solar generation is low.

Loi 1: Doan theo kien thuc

Ban biet coffee den tu Ethiopia nhung passage noi khac — phai theo passage, khong theo kien thuc.

Loi 2: Nham NG voi False

False can bang chung trai nguoc. NG la KHONG co bang chung nao. Neu ban khong tim thay gi — do la NG.

Loi 3: Bo qua tu khoa

"Most" vs "all", "sometimes" vs "always", "many" vs "every" — nhung tu nho nay thay doi dap an hoan toan.

Loi 4: Doc qua nhanh

Phai doc ky 2-3 cau quanh tu khoa. Dung chi doc 1 tu roi ket luan.
