In vs At vs On

  • IN: thang, nam, mua, nuoc, thanh pho — in May, in 2024, in Vietnam, in the morning
  • AT: gio, dia diem cu the, su kien — at 9 AM, at the station, at the party
  • ON: ngay, thu, be mat — on Monday, on my birthday, on the table

De nham: At vs In

  • arrive at the airport (dia diem cu the)
  • arrive in London (thanh pho/nuoc)
  • in the car (xe hoi)
  • on the bus/train/plane (phuong tien cong cong)

Loi hay gap

  • Hoc gioi tu bang cach dich may moc.
  • Nham giua at/in/on/of/for.
  • Khong hoc theo cum di kem.
  • Quen ngu canh su dung tu nhien.
  • Dung "depend of" thay vi "depend on".

Vi sao quan trong

  • Rat hay sai trong speaking va writing.
  • Giup cau tu nhien hon ngay lap tuc.
  • La nen de mo rong collocation va phrasal verbs.
  • IELTS Writing thuong tru diem vi sai gioi tu.
